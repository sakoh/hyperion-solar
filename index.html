Why Solar Energy?

What does solar energy provide compared to other energy sources?

Though fossil fuels, such as coal, oil, natural gas, makes up a majority of the world's current electricity supply is generated, these fossil energy sources are becomingly increasely problematic, presenting challenges such as rising prices, security concerns over dependence on imports from a limited number of countries which control most of the fossil fuel supplies, and increasing risks to the environment.

In response to these challenges presented by fossil fuels, increasing govenments, private businesses, and individuals are turning to alternative and renewable energy sources, chief among them being solar energy. The advantage that solar presents over fossil fuel is that it is based a renewable energy source, which is the sun.
